AI Project1: Music Generator

Eleanor Wright - Coordinator
Gray Leonard - Implementor
Sean Yang - Documentor

Overview

Our goal for this project was to create an AI that generates samples of music based on the user's emotional preferences (happy or sad). Originally, we were trying to get more options for user preferences, but we decided to use user preferences of happy or sad because it was easier to evaluate.
Here are two examples:
1. Happy Song
2. Sad Song

Design and Technical Approach

For this AI project, we used hierarchical Markov chains to create music from existing midi files. (For more information on how we implemented Hierarchical Markov Chains to create music, read page 2 of Project Report.
We used Git to share our source codes because we were able to edit, review, import and export documents easily among our group members.

Evaluation

We did a survey using a sample of happy and sample of sad song. About 96% said that the happy song was happy and 80% thought the sad song was sad; therefor, we have a successful AI. Because the music generator is somewhat random, it is difficult to create a music generator AI that can mimic everyone's interpretations of music, but we successfully manage to create a decent AI.
